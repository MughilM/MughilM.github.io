## Project Euler

Project Euler is a constantly updating database of mathematical puzzles that are designed to be done at the aid of programming. Most problems revolve interesting number theory topics i.e. prime numbers, divisibility. Currently there are 600+ problems on the docket, ranging from fairly simple, to excruciatingly difficult.

In terms of how fast your solution needs to be, the website recommends solving the problem within **one minute.** To get it within that time, it is more than likely that prior mathematical knowledge is needed to make the problem efficient.

As I solve the problems, I will updating a blog with the solution to each problem. I plan to take a "math-first" approach, rather than a "programming-first" approach, because I view as understanding the maths behind each problem more important. Of course, if the solution is purely algorithmic, I plan to explain it properly.

Check back for updates regularly!
